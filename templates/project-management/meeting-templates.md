# Meeting Templates

---

## Daily Standup (15 minutes)

### Format
Each team member answers 3 questions:

1. **Yesterday**: What did I accomplish?
2. **Today**: What will I work on?
3. **Blockers**: Any impediments?

**Date**: [DATE]

| Name | Yesterday | Today | Blockers |

| [Name] | | | |

- [ ] [Action] - [Owner] - [Deadline]

## Sprint Planning (2 hours)

### Agenda

1. Review Sprint Goal (10 min)
2. Review capacity & velocity (10 min)
3. Story selection & estimation (60 min)
4. Task breakdown (30 min)
5. Commitment & wrap-up (10 min)
